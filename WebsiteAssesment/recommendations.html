20" Paiste Power Ride Alpha

The Paiste Alpha series are considered to be some of the best cymbals when it comes to a balance of sound and price, they sound professional whilst remaining within the budget of most drummer. The 20" Power Ride is a great option from the alpha series if your looking for a powerful ride that can cut through the mix of a band. the alpha series was discontinued in 2017 but used cymbals are still around. They no longer make packs for the alpha series. They were replaced with the 900 series which is another great option if your looking for afforadble cymbals that sound great. It has a bright, clear sound with a strong ping and a defined bell, making it perfect for rock and metal music. Its durability and affordability make it a great choice for drummers of all skill levels. I personally use the power ride in my main kit as it is one of the best cymbals I have ever played in that price range.

Meinl HCS Cymbal Set

The Meinl HCS cymbal set is one of the most versatile and accessible beginner cymbal lines available from any brand. While they are by no means professional-grade cymbals, they offer a very affordable option for new drummers who are just getting started. They're made from brass, which is a much cheaper material than the bronze used in higher-end cymbals, and that means they don't have the same warmth, complexity, or durability. However, most beginners won't notice the difference in sound quality, especially when just learning the basics of drumming. The HCS line comes in a wide variety of pre-packaged sets and individual cymbals, with some of the biggest sets including up to 20 different pieces. This makes it easy for beginners to experiment with different sounds and setups without spending a lot of money. The cymbals themselves have a bright and cutting tone, which can help them stand out in a beginner kit, especially in smaller practice spaces or low-volume environments. While more experienced drummers will likely outgrow these quickly, the HCS line is a great way to get started without breaking the bank, and they're perfect for early practice, home recording, or casual jamming.

Paiste RUDE

The Paiste RUDE cymbals are a bold and aggressive line designed specifically for loud, heavy styles like punk, metal, hardcore, and hard rock. They're not made to be subtle or smooth—instead, they're all about raw power, cutting attack, and maximum volume. Made from B8 bronze, the RUDE series is incredibly durable and built to withstand heavy playing without cracking or losing tone. Their unlathed, raw finish gives them a unique look and contributes to their sharp, piercing sound that easily slices through dense mixes and loud guitars. While they definitely aren't the most versatile cymbals and can sound too harsh or trashy for softer genres, that's not what they're made for. If you're a hard-hitting drummer who needs cymbals that can match your intensity and volume, the Paiste RUDE line delivers exactly that, no frills, just power.

Meinl Byzance Series

The Meinl Byzance series is one of the most respected and sought-after cymbal lines in the professional drumming world, known for its exceptional craftsmanship, musicality, and tonal variety. Each cymbal in the Byzance line is made from B20 bronze and hand-hammered in Turkey, giving them a unique, organic character that stands out in any musical setting. What sets the Byzance series apart is its wide range of sub-series, each tailored to different styles and preferences—Byzance Dark for moody, dry tones; Vintage for warm, washy textures; Brilliant for a polished, bright sound; Extra Dry for super controlled, earthy tones; and more. This versatility makes Byzance a favorite among drummers across genres, from jazz and fusion to metal, funk, and progressive rock. These cymbals are not only sonically rich, with deep complexity and dynamic response, but also visually striking due to their raw or finely finished surfaces. Whether you're playing intricate studio work or loud live gigs, the Byzance series delivers nuanced tones that respond well to both soft touches and aggressive playing. For drummers looking for top-tier cymbals with character, depth, and serious professional quality, Meinl Byzance is one of the best choices available.

Zildjian K Custom Series

The Zildjian K Custom series is a top-tier line of professional cymbals known for their dark, warm tones and exceptional versatility. Designed for drummers who need both subtlety and power, K Customs are perfect for genres like jazz, fusion, rock, and even more aggressive styles when needed. Made from Zildjian's signature B20 bronze and carefully hand-hammered, each cymbal in the series offers rich, complex tones with a smooth, musical character. What makes the K Custom line stand out is its wide variety of models, such as the K Custom Dark, Hybrid, and Special Dry, each offering unique textures from dry and controlled to bright and responsive. These cymbals shine in both studio and live settings, delivering a level of tone and dynamic expression that seasoned drummers rely on. Whether you're playing intricate jazz patterns or laying into a rock groove, the K Custom series provides the clarity, depth, and feel that make them a favorite among professionals worldwide.
