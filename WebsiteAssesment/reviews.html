Drumkit Reviews

This is the drumkit of Eloy Casagrande, the former drummer of the Brazilian metal band Sepultura and current drummer of nu-metal band slipknot. It is a custom-made kit hand picked by eloy and it consists of Tama Starclassic Bubinga drums, Paiste cymbals, Evans drumheads, Pro-Mark drumsticks, and Gibraltar hardware. The kit features four bass drums two 22"x16" and two 24"x14, three rack toms 10"x7", 12"x8", and 13"x9", two floor toms 6"x14" and 18"x16", a snare drum 14"x6" (PBB146), and a side snare 14"x6.5" (BB156). On top of all this he has a absolutely stacked amount of cymbals including a Formula 602 Modern Essentials Ride, a Formula 602 Modern Essentials Crash, a RUDE Blast China, a PST X Swiss Flanger Stack, two more Formula 602 Modern Essentials Crashes, and the Paiste 15" Formula 602 Modern Essentials Hi-Hat. This isnt even all of his cymbals, his full kit contains accents and splash cymbals as well. Behind the kit is a 40" gong.
